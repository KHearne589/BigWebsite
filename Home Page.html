Anime is believed to just be nothing more than a simple "Japanese cartoon", to those of us who watch it, it is far more than that, it is our way of life. Anime is just something so unique and entertaining, that it is able to express so many different things that an ordinary TV show cannot express, because of it's extenisive use of animation. As they say, "a picture is worth a thousand words" and through amazing graphics and animation, anime sends across a much greater message. Since anime is animated, it can depict various types of characters and situations which normally, actors may not be willing to do. It breaks several barriers and allows room for better humour, better depth of characters and overall, an amazing viewing experience.


Of course many people will think differently to this, but this is the opinion of many anime fans, otakus and weaboos who love everything that anime has given us, the culture we follow and can celebrate with many others of our kind at conventions or sudden meetings in public.
