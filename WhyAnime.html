I chose to create this website surrounding anime because, it is something that as stuck with me since childhood and although people hate on anime and call it childish, I know I can prove them wrong, and I can still enjoy the lists on lists of brilliant anime, new or old it was still something I enjoyed every moment of watching and still will for a long time, no matter what people around me may say. I remember some of my first anime I watched like Sword Art Online or Gurren Lagann, I wasn't exactly sure what I was watching at first but the more I watched it, I just wanted to continue, and so I still am today, enticed with watching every heartwarming slice of life to Hilariously funny comedy anime.

One of the great things about anime is that it most likely will never end, it will continue to grow, and improve and draw in a larger audience as the anime community is still ever growing. And with numerous different genres of anime like, action, comedy, romance, slice of life, sports, mecha and many more, theres always something to interest any of the viewers.
